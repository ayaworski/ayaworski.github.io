Wholesale Marketplace: From Closed to Connected

How I defined and executed a strategic vision to transform B2B buying into a connected, discovery-driven marketplace.

Project Overview

I led the vision, strategy, and execution for Lightspeed's Wholesale Marketplace. By defining the opportunity, aligning cross-functional teams, and evolving our design system in real time, I delivered a foundational experience that transformed the B2B buying experience and accelerated network-driven growth.

The Problem

The existing wholesale model operates as a closed network. Merchants can only browse and purchase from brands they already know. This siloed experience limits discovery, constrains new brand‚Äìmerchant relationships, and reduces the overall value potential of the B2B network.

Opportunity

Enable open access across the wholesale ecosystem so merchants can discover new brands, brands can reach new buyers, and the platform can surface more of the network's inherent value. A more connected discovery and purchasing model would directly expand choice, strengthen relationships, and create new revenue pathways.

How might we create a wholesale marketplace that broadens discovery and accelerates high-value brand‚Äìmerchant connections across the network?

Design Approach

We ran a three-week design spike to rapidly turn ambiguity into a clear vision. A small, cross-functional team defined goals, constraints, and success criteria, then used high-fidelity prototypes to test assumptions, uncover insights, and align stakeholders on the path forward.

Cross-Functional Collaboration

To unpack the problem and align the team on a strategic path forward, I led a focused discovery sprint to clarify opportunities, validate assumptions, and reinforce business objectives.

To maintain alignment and momentum, the team collaborated in person and held twice-daily check-ins. Frequent visibility helped guide iterations, strengthened shared accountability, and ensured decisions reflected both user needs and business goals.

Strategic Leadership

When direction started drifting away from solving for the complexities of B2B brand-buyer relationships, I stepped up as a strategic leader. I reframed the work to move beyond a simple open shopping experience toward a solution that both supports existing brand-merchant relationships and facilitates new ones.

Design & Validation

With a clear strategy in place, I led the team in shaping prototypes that brought our vision to life. By anchoring the story to our ICP, I guided senior stakeholders through a narrative demonstrating how the future marketplace would function:

  • Cross-brand product discovery
  • Initiating new brand-buyer relationships
  • Comparing and evaluating products
  • Confident multi-supplier ordering
  • Scalability across verticals and retailer types

Interactive prototypes and clear storytelling made the vision tangible and drove the discussions that shaped next steps.

Roadmap & Alignment

By turning strategy into a clear visual narrative, I accelerated stakeholder alignment, surfaced actionable feedback, and secured leadership commitment. This clarified priorities and established a unified path forward.

Execution & Impact

With the roadmap defined, I translated the vision into actionable next steps for the team, balancing long-term strategy with the need for rapid MVP delivery. I worked cross-functionally to navigate shifting priorities, unblock teams, and maintain alignment.

Despite an aggressive five-month timeline, we successfully delivered the marketplace MVP. By shipping a high-quality experience quickly, we unlocked immediate value for buyers and sellers and established the foundation for ongoing iterations that continue to expand the marketplace‚Äôs impact.

From my perspective, you understood the urgency, translated the future vision into actionable MVP designs, and worked iteratively with the team to keep us unblocked.

Driving Scalable Growth

During the delivery of the Marketplace, I faced the challenge of building within a nascent design system. I partnered closely with the Systems team to evolve components in real time, balancing immediate needs with long-term system priorities.

Product cards and related UI were reverse-engineered into functional components that the systems team adopted for company-wide use.

Business Impact

The Marketplace delivery is a foundational and strategic differentiator for Lightspeed. It enables retailers to discover new brands, diversify assortments, and manage wholesale workflows directly within the POS.

Marketplace is the next evolution of our wholesale network. It connects brands and retailers to the right products at the right moment and helps them buy and sell smarter within a unified retail ecosystem.

Founder and CEO of Lightspeed Commerce

Dax Dasilva

Reflection
